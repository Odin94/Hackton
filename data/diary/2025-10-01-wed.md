Antisymmetry: if aRb and bRa, then a=b. Asymmetry: if aRb then NOT bRa. The difference is that asymmetric relations can never have aRa, while antisymmetric ones can. I understand this when I read it slowly. Under any time pressure I revert to confusing them.
