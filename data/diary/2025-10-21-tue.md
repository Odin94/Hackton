Continued with graph representations — adjacency lists vs adjacency matrices. The trade-off is the classic space vs time pattern: matrices give O(1) edge lookup but O(V²) space, lists give O(degree) lookup but O(V+E) space. For sparse graphs (most real networks, including neural network layer connectivity) lists win. I understood this immediately from ML context.
