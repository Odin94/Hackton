**Morning**

Analysis lecture: sequences and series in more depth. The ratio test is becoming automatic — compute the limit of |a_{n+1}/a_n|, if it's less than 1 the series converges, greater than 1 it diverges, equal to 1 it's inconclusive. The root test is similar but takes the nth root instead.

The comparison test is the one I don't have clean instincts for yet. You need to find a series you already know converges or diverges and compare your series to it. The hard part is picking the right comparison — it requires pattern recognition that I haven't built yet.
