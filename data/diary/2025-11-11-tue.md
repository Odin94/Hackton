**Evening**

Induction proof practice. One proof, timed. Proved that the sum of the first n odd numbers equals n². Base case: 1² = 1. Inductive step: assuming it holds for k, show it for k+1. Add the next odd number (2k+1) to both sides. k² + (2k+1) = (k+1)². Done in 12 minutes, clean.

Six weeks ago I would have stared at that for 30 minutes. Progress is real.
